Life can at times be difficult, especially when we are going through times of transition which require us to make changes. The teenage years can be a bit of a rollercoaster, for both parents and the teen. But if you lay the groundwork —by giving your teen the skills they needs to make good decisions—the teen years don’t have to be tumultuous. Sometimes you might feel like you’ve taken one step forward and two steps backward in terms of your teen’s progress.

Adolescent children have different kinds of problems. Parents don’t realize when their children have entered in Adolescence after finishing their childhood, because the changes in teenagers are very normal and natural but parents generally don’t realize or understand, accept and deal with these changes. Many children are not aware of the effects of hormonal changes, most of the time even parents are not aware of it. Parents feel that development of their adolescent children should be proper but they are confused about the direction.
